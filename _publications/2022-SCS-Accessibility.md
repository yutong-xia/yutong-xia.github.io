Taking Wuhan, China, as the study area, this paper measured the hourly accessibility to healthcare by using two methods: Gaussian Two-Step Floating Catchment Area and Weighted Average Travel Time. Then, it investigated how traffic conditions affected the equality of healthcare services among places or demographic groups at different times.
